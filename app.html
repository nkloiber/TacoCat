TacoCat: A Coding Challenge

TacoCat is a palindrome. A palindrome is a word or phrase that reads out the same regardless of whether it's read front-to-back or back-to-front. Don't understand it? That's fine! This app was built to check if any word is a palindrome or not. Just throw in a word and it'll tell you whether or not it fits the criteria, and if it doesn't, it'll still reverse it for you either way.


Some popular palindromes are...

- Racecar
- Taco Cat
- Madam, I'm Adam
- Never odd or even
- A man, a plan, a canal- Panama
